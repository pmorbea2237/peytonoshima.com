ABOUT PEYTON

Peyton Oshima began karting in 2013 at the age of 8, competing at club level in Japan. After proving his talent in karting, he transitioned to single-seaters in 2020 at 15, quickly making a name for himself in junior formula. He was inducted into the Scuderia Ferrari Driver Academy. His breakthrough came in 2022 when he claimed the FIA Formula 3 title, followed by a strong FIA Formula 2 campaign in 2024. After 3 race starts as a reserve driver in 2024, Peyton will step up as a full-time Ferrari Formula 1 driver in 2025.
