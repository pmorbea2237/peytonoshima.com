ABOUT

PEYTON

Peyton Oshima began karting in 2013 at the age of 8, competing at club level in Japan. After proving his talent in karting, he transitioned to single-seaters in 2020 at 15, quickly making a name for himself in junior formula. His breakthrough came in 2022 when he claimed the FIA Formula 3 title, followed by a strong FIA Formula 2 campaign in 2023. As a result, he was inducted into the Scuderia Ferrari Driver Academy.

After serving as Scuderia Ferrari's reserve driver and making 3 race starts in 2024, Peyton will step up as a full-time Ferrari Formula 1 driver for the 2025 season.
